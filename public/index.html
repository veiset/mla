Music Label Agency is an independent record label that publishes music performed by music performers. At Music Label Agency, we endavour to publish only music performed by music performers.

Music performers such as the "Music Performers" and "T and the Dawgs" are currently signed to the Music Label Agency label.

Look and listen for music published by the Music Label Agency record label at locations where music is performed or published.
